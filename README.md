## Hi there 👋

# 💫 About Me:
I'm a brand & visual designer with a background spanning international agencies, product consulting, and entrepreneurship. I work at the intersection of brand identity, UI design, and communication design.<br>I care deeply about designing for impactful projects, things that work without me. I make sure that my creations are accessible, and usable by anyone.<br><br>I apply that same principle to open source: I'm currently building the visual identity and website for Paye Pas Pour Ça, a pro bono platform making free legal resources accessible to foreign nationals in France.<br>When I'm not designing, I'm running hands-on science workshops to help people understand their afro hair (and yes, that involves microscopes.)
